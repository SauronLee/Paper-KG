UnImplicit Shared Task Report: Detecting Clarification Requirements in Instructional Text | Michael Roth | this paper describes the data , task setup , and results of the shared task at the first workshop on understanding implicit and underspecified language \( unimplicit \) . the task requires computational models to predict whether a sentence contains aspects of meaning that are contextually unspecified and thus require clarification. two teams participated and the best scoring system achieved an accuracy of 68%.
