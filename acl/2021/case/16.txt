SU-NLP at CASE 2021 Task 1: Protest News Detection for English | Furkan Çelik | this paper summarizes our group’s efforts in the multilingual protest news detection shared task , which is organized as a part of the challenges and applications of automated extraction of socio-political events from text \( case \) workshop. we participated in all four subtasks in english. especially in the identification of event containing sentences task , our proposed ensemble approach using roberta and multichannel cnn-lexstem model yields higher performance. similarly in the event extraction task , our transformer-lstm-crf architecture outperforms regular transformers significantly.
