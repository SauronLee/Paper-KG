macech at SemEval-2021 Task 5: Toxic Spans Detection | Maggie Cech | toxic language is often present in online forums , especially when politics and other polarizing topics arise , and can lead to people becoming discouraged from joining or continuing conversations. in this paper , we use data consisting of comments with the indices of toxic text labelled to train an rnn to deter-mine which parts of the comments make them toxic , which could aid online moderators. we compare results using both the original dataset and an augmented set , as well as gru versus lstm rnn models.
