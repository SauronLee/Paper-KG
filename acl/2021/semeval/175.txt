CONNER: A Cascade Count and Measurement Extraction Tool for Scientific Discourse | Jiarun Cao | this paper presents our wining contribution to semeval 2021 task 8: measeval. the purpose of this task is identifying the counts and measurements from clinical scientific discourse , including quantities , entities , properties , qualifiers , units , modifiers , and their mutual relations. this task can be induced to a joint entity and relation extraction problem. accordingly , we propose conner , a cascade count and measurement extraction tool that can identify entities and the corresponding relations in a two-step pipeline model. we provide a detailed description of the proposed model hereinafter. furthermore , the impact of the essential modules and our in-process technical schemes are also investigated.
