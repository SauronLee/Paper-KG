AttesTable at SemEval-2021 Task 9: Extending Statement Verification with Tables for Unknown Class, and Semantic Evidence Finding | Harshit Varma | this paper describes our approach for task 9 of semeval 2021: statement verification and evidence finding with tables. we participated in both subtasks , namely statement verification and evidence finding. for the subtask of statement verification , we extend the tapas model to adapt to the ‘unknown’ class of statements by finetuning it on an augmented version of the task data. for the subtask of evidence finding , we finetune the distilbert model in a siamese setting.
