FII_CROSS at SemEval-2021 Task 2: Multilingual and Cross-lingual Word-in-Context Disambiguation | Ciprian Bodnar | this paper presents a word-in-context disambiguation system. the task focuses on capturing the polysemous nature of words in a multilingual and cross-lingual setting , without considering a strict inventory of word meanings. the system applies natural language processing algorithms on datasets from semeval 2021 task 2 , being able to identify the meaning of words for the languages arabic , chinese , english , french and russian , without making use of any additional mono- or multilingual resources.
