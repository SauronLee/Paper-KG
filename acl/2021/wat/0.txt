Overview of the 8th Workshop on Asian Translation | Toshiaki Nakazawa | this paper presents the results of the shared tasks from the 8th workshop on asian translation \( wat2021 \) . for the wat2021 , 28 teams participated in the shared tasks and 24 teams submitted their translation results for the human evaluation. we also accepted 5 research papers. about 2 , 100 translation results were submitted to the automatic evaluation server , and selected submissions were manually evaluated.
