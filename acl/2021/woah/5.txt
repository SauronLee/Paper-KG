DALC: the Dutch Abusive Language Corpus | Tommaso Caselli | as socially unacceptable language become pervasive in social media platforms , the need for automatic content moderation become more pressing. this contribution introduces the dutch abusive language corpus \( dalc v1.0 \) , a new dataset with tweets manually an- notated for abusive language. the resource ad- dress a gap in language resources for dutch and adopts a multi-layer annotation scheme modeling the explicitness and the target of the abusive messages. baselines experiments on all annotation layers have been conducted , achieving a macro f1 score of 0.748 for binary classification of the explicitness layer and .489 for target classification.
