Findings of the WOAH 5 Shared Task on Fine Grained Hateful Memes Detection | Lambert Mathias | we present the results and main findings of the shared task at woah 5 on hateful memes detection. the task include two subtasks relating to distinct challenges in the fine-grained detection of hateful memes: \( 1 \) the protected category attacked by the meme and \( 2 \) the attack type. 3 teams submitted system description papers. this shared task builds on the hateful memes detection task created by facebook ai research in 2020.
