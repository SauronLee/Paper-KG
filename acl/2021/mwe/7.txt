Light Verb Constructions and Their Families - A Corpus Study on German ‘stehen unter’-LVCs | Jens Fleischhauer | the paper reports on a corpus study of german light verb constructions \( lvcs \) . lvcs come in families which exemplify systematic interpretation patterns. the paper’s aim is to account for the properties determining these patterns on the basis of a corpus study on german lvcs of the type ‘stehen unter’ np’ \( ‘stand under np’ \) .
