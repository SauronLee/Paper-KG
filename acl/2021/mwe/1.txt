Where Do Aspectual Variants of Light Verb Constructions Belong? | Aggeliki Fotopoulou | expressions with an aspectual variant of a light verb , e.g. ‘take on debt’ vs. ‘have debt’ , are frequent in texts but often difficult to classify between verbal idioms , light verb constructions or compositional phrases. we investigate the properties of such expressions with a disputed membership and propose a selection of features that determine more satisfactory boundaries between the three categories in this zone , assigning the expressions to one of them.
