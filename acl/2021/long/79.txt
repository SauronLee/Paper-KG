VoxPopuli: A Large-Scale Multilingual Speech Corpus for Representation Learning, Semi-Supervised Learning and Interpretation | Changhan Wang | we introduce voxpopuli , a large-scale multilingual corpus providing 400k hours of unlabeled speech data in 23 languages. it is the largest open data to date for unsupervised representation learning as well as semi-supervised learning. voxpopuli also contains 1.8k hours of transcribed speeches in 15 languages and their aligned oral interpretations into 15 target languages totaling 17.3k hours. we provide speech recognition \( asr \) baselines and validate the versatility of voxpopuli unlabeled data in semi-supervised asr and speech-to-text translation under challenging out-of-domain settings. the corpus is available at https://github.com/facebookresearch/voxpopuli.
