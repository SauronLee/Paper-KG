CraftAssist Instruction Parsing: Semantic Parsing for a Voxel-World Assistant | Kavya Srinet | we propose a semantic parsing dataset focused on instruction-driven communication with an agent in the game minecraft. the dataset consists of 7k human utterances and their corresponding parses. given proper world state , the parses can be interpreted and executed in game. we report the performance of baseline models , and analyze their successes and failures.
