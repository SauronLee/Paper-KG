Xiaomi’s Submissions for IWSLT 2020 Open Domain Translation Task | Yuhui Sun | this paper describes the xiaomi’s submissions to the iwslt20 shared open domain translation task for chinese<->japanese language pair. we explore different model ensembling strategies based on recent transformer variants. we also further strengthen our systems via some effective techniques , such as data filtering , data selection , tagged back translation , domain adaptation , knowledge distillation , and re-ranking. our resulting chinese->japanese primary system ranked second in terms of character-level bleu score among all submissions. our resulting japanese->chinese primary system also achieved a competitive performance.
