Grapheme-to-Phoneme Conversion with a Multilingual Transformer Model | Omnia ElSaadany | in this paper , we describe our three submissions to the sigmorphon 2020 shared task 1 on grapheme-to-phoneme conversion for 15 languages. we experimented with a single multilingual transformer model. we observed that the multilingual model achieves results on par with our separately trained monolingual models and is even able to avoid a few of the errors made by the monolingual models.
