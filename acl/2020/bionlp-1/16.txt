Domain Adaptation and Instance Selection for Disease Syndrome Classification over Veterinary Clinical Notes | Brian Hur | identifying the reasons for antibiotic administration in veterinary records is a critical component of understanding antimicrobial usage patterns. this informs antimicrobial stewardship programs designed to fight antimicrobial resistance , a major health crisis affecting both humans and animals in which veterinarians have an important role to play. we propose a document classification approach to determine the reason for administration of a given drug , with particular focus on domain adaptation from one drug to another , and instance selection to minimize annotation effort.
