Estimating the effect of COVID-19 on mental health: Linguistic indicators of depression during a global pandemic | JT Wolohan | this preliminary analysis uses a deep lstm neural network with fasttext embeddings to predict population rates of depression on reddit in order to estimate the effect of covid-19 on mental health. we find that year over year , depression rates on reddit are up 50% , suggesting a 15-million person increase in the number of depressed americans and a $7.5 billion increase in depression related spending. this finding suggests that utility in nlp approaches to longitudinal public-health surveillance.
